# Expense Policy

Travel expenses are reimbursed when they are approved by a manager before the trip. Receipts are required for airfare, lodging, ground transportation, and meals.

Lunch expenses during normal office days are not reimbursed. Meals are reimbursed only during approved business travel or approved customer meetings.

Software subscriptions may be expensed when they support active project work and cost less than 50 USD per month. Higher-cost tools require finance approval before purchase.
