# Remote Work Addendum

The general handbook says remote work is allowed up to two days per week when the employee's manager approves the schedule. Employees should keep their calendar updated and attend required team meetings.

The infrastructure operations team has a stricter rule during launch weeks. During a launch week, remote work is prohibited for on-call infrastructure engineers unless the director grants a written exception.

Contractors may work remotely when their contract explicitly allows it. If the contract is silent, the default expectation is on-site work for security-sensitive projects.
